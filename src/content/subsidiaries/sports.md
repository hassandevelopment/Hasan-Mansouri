Hasan Mansouri Sports is the authorised distributor for Precor Products Ltd. (USA) in Bahrain. Precor's cardio and strength equipment is world-class — exceptionally solid and durable, with low maintenance requirements.

## Products & Services

The division supplies and installs the full range of Precor fitness equipment to hotels, health clubs, gyms, and private clients throughout Bahrain. All installations are supported by professional after-sales service, and equipment carries a one-year warranty on parts and labour.

## Membership

Hasan Mansouri Sports is a registered member of the IDEA Health & Fitness Association, reflecting the division's commitment to professional standards in the fitness industry.
