Tiffany's is a distinctive dining destination located directly opposite Mansouri Mansions Hotel in Adliya, Manama. The restaurant is illuminated by hand-crafted Tiffany lamps and fitted with marble flooring and crafted walnut furniture, creating an atmosphere of warmth and elegance.

## Dining Experience

The menu caters to every occasion — from leisurely lunch sandwiches and casual drinks at the bar, to three and four-course candlelit dinners accompanied by a curated selection of new-world wines.

Ambient music featuring Dean Martin, Ella Fitzgerald, and Frank Sinatra sets a timeless tone throughout the evening.
