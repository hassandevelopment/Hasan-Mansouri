Recognised as one of the leading steel fabrication companies in the Kingdom of Bahrain, Hasan Mansouri Engineering operates a 40,000 sq.m facility equipped with industrial machinery, advanced technology, and qualified engineers using the latest design software.

## Services

The division supplies fabricated, galvanised, and painted steel structures including steel buildings, industrial ducting, pipe racks, platforms, handrails, ladders, stair stringers, and stair treads.

## Major Projects

- **Batelco Building Phase I & II** — Structural steelwork for Bahrain's national telecommunications headquarters
- **Diplomatic Area** — Commercial district structural works
- **BIA Terminal Building Extension** — Structural steelwork for Bahrain International Airport terminal expansion
- **ALBA Potline 4 Extension** — Approximately 3,600 tonnes of steel for one of the Gulf's largest aluminium smelters
- **National Bank of Bahrain Headquarters** — Supply, detail design, fabrication, and erection of structural steelwork for the 26-storey headquarters building
