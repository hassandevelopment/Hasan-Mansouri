Hasan Mansouri Properties develops and leases residential and commercial real estate throughout the Kingdom of Bahrain. The portfolio spans gated compounds, apartments, villas, and office spaces in prime locations.

## Portfolio

**Kuranah Garden — Budaiya**  
A landscaped residential compound offering privacy and comfort.

**Mariposa Garden — Sehla**  
A sought-after compound with garden setting and full amenities.

**Burhama Properties**  
A selection of villas and bungalows in a well-established residential district.

## Amenities

All residential properties are developed with occupant wellbeing in mind. Standard amenities across the portfolio include swimming pools, tennis courts, picnic areas, private balconies and patios, maid's rooms, private garages, guest parking, and 24-hour emergency maintenance.
