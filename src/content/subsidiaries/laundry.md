Hasan Mansouri Laundry is a commercial laundry and dry-cleaning operation based in Salmabad, Bahrain. For over 25 years, the division has provided high-quality laundry services to some of the Kingdom's most demanding institutional clients.

## Clients

- American Mission Hospital
- Bahrain International
- The Country Club
- Mansouri Mansions Hotel
- La Fontaine

## Capabilities

The facility operates a complete range of American laundry equipment and is staffed by a professional laundry team trained to maintain consistent, high-quality output at scale. Services cover commercial laundry, dry cleaning, and specialist garment care.
