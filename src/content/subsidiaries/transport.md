Hasan Mansouri Transport provides hire of cranes, trailers, and heavy construction equipment to major industrial and government clients across the Kingdom of Bahrain.

## Services

The division supplies a full range of heavy lifting and transport equipment, including mobile cranes, low-loaders, and specialist trailers. All equipment is maintained to the highest standard, with operators fully trained in health, safety, and environmental requirements.

## Clients

Hasan Mansouri Transport supplies equipment to ALBA, BAPCO, ASRY, the US Navy, Costain, and many other leading industrial operators in Bahrain. All cranes are tested and certified in accordance with international safety standards.
