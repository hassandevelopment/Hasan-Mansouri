Mansouri Mansions Hotel is a four-star establishment in Adliya, Manama, operating since 1979. The hotel comprises one, two and three bedroom apartments with equipped kitchens, lounges, and en-suite bathrooms — ideal for business travellers and long-stay guests.

Adjoining Mansouri Mansions Apartments provides luxury accommodation for long-term residents seeking the service standards of a hotel with the comfort of a private home.

## Facilities

- Sizzlers Restaurant
- Henry's Café Bar
- Tramps Night Club
- The Press Wine Bar
- Pool Bar
- 24-hour Fitness Centre
- Outdoor Swimming Pool
- 24-hour Home Delivery Service
